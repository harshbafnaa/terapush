Editorial Guidelines

We publish original
thinking. Not press releases.

The Terapush blog is read by founders, investors, and operators across India's startup ecosystem. We hold submissions to a high editorial bar — not in length, but in substance.

We'll review your submission and come back to you within 5 business days. Accepted articles go through a light editorial pass and are published under your byline.

01
Original and unpublishedWe only publish work that hasn't appeared elsewhere. No reposts or cross-publishes.
02
800–2,500 wordsLong enough to say something real, short enough to respect the reader's time.
03
A specific point of viewNo listicles that could apply to any market. Tell us what you've seen, done, or learned — specifically.
04
India-relevant contextOur readers are building and investing in Bharat. Ground your piece in this reality.
05
No paid promotionWe don't publish sponsored content or advertorials. Disclosure of any affiliation is mandatory.
