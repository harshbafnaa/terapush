FAQs

How can startups connect with investors?

Startups can create profiles and reach out to investors through our platform.

Can investors access due diligence support?

Yes, investors can request reports before funding decisions.

How do service providers find projects?

They can browse listings, submit proposals, and collaborate with startups.

Are there opportunities for job seekers?

Yes, they can apply for positions and network with startups.
