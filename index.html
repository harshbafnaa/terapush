About Terapush

Terapush was founded in 2025 with a clear vision: to create a dynamic, interconnected ecosystem where innovation thrives. We observed a significant gap in the market where promising startups struggled to find the right funding, talent, and support, while investors sought vetted opportunities, and skilled professionals desired meaningful roles within the vibrant startup world. Our platform is designed to bridge these gaps, offering a comprehensive suite of tools and networking capabilities that empower every participant to achieve their goals.

We believe in the power of connection and collaboration. By bringing together diverse stakeholders, Terapush cultivates an environment where ideas transform into successful ventures, careers are launched, and service providers become integral partners in the journey of growth. We are committed to fostering transparency, efficiency, and mutual success across the entire startup landscape.

Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of startups can join Terapush?
Terapush welcomes startups from all industries and stages, from early-seed to Series A and beyond. Our platform is designed to cater to a diverse range of innovative ventures.
How does Terapush help investors find suitable startups?
Investors can utilize advanced filters to discover startups based on industry, funding stage, traction, and more. We also provide curated introductions and due diligence support to facilitate informed decisions.
Is there a cost associated with using Terapush?
Terapush offers various membership tiers, including free access for basic features and premium subscriptions with enhanced benefits for startups, investors, and service providers. Specific pricing details are available upon joining.
How does Terapush ensure the quality of service providers?
Service providers on Terapush undergo a vetting process and are encouraged to showcase their portfolios and client testimonials. Our platform also allows for ratings and reviews from startups they've worked with.
What kind of job opportunities are available for job seekers?
Job seekers can find a wide array of roles, including full-time, part-time, internship, and freelance positions across various departments like tech, marketing, operations, and design within our network of startups.
