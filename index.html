FAQs

How can startups connect with investors?

Startups can create a profile, showcase their business, and reach out to interested investors through our platform.

Can investors access due diligence support?

Yes, investors can request due diligence reports and insights on startups before making funding decisions.

How do service providers find projects?

Service providers can browse startup listings, submit proposals, and collaborate on initiatives directly on Terapush.

Are there opportunities for job seekers?

Job seekers can apply for full-time or freelance positions, connect with startups, and enhance their professional network.
