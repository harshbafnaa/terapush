What We're Building

Services &
Platform

Terapush is a multi-layered platform — part professional network, part marketplace, part intelligence feed. Here's what we're building and why it matters.

01
Professional
Network
Community
A curated professional network designed specifically for India's startup ecosystem. Unlike generic platforms where signal is drowned by noise, Terapush surfaces the right people at the right time.
  • Verified founder, investor, and operator profiles
  • Smart matching based on sector, stage, and intent
  • Direct introductions and warm referrals
  • Public thought leadership and content sharing
02
Deal
Marketplace
Commerce
A structured marketplace where founders discover service providers, co-founders, advisors, and strategic partners — all vetted within the Terapush ecosystem.
  • Curated listings for legal, finance, tech, and marketing services
  • Co-founder matching with compatibility signals
  • Advisor marketplace with transparent rate cards
  • Deal room infrastructure for structured negotiations
03
Capital
Discovery
Funding
A two-sided discovery layer connecting startups with relevant investors — without cold emails, middlemen, or pay-to-play visibility. Founders get found; investors find what they're looking for.
  • Investor profiles with public investment thesis and portfolio
  • Stage-matched deal flow for angel, seed, and Series A investors
  • Verified fundraising status and traction signals for founders
  • Direct outreach tools with request-to-connect protocols
04
Startup
Intelligence
Insights
A curated intelligence feed tailored to your sector, stage, and interests — surfacing funding announcements, regulatory changes, market signals, and ecosystem news that actually matters.
  • Daily briefings filtered by sector and stage
  • Funding round announcements and investor moves
  • Regulatory and policy updates for Indian startups
  • Community-sourced insights and analysis
05
Community
Rooms
Dialogue
Structured discussion spaces organized by sector, theme, and intent. A place for founders to ask real questions, share real answers, and build genuine peer relationships.
  • Sector rooms: Fintech, D2C, SaaS, HealthTech, and more
  • Topic rooms: Fundraising, Hiring, Legal, Growth
  • AMA sessions with founders and investors
  • Mentorship matching within communities
06
Founder
Reputation
Identity
Your verified track record as a builder — past ventures, roles, wins, and expertise. Think of it as your startup CV, but one that the ecosystem can actually see and trust.
  • Verified founding history and exit records
  • Peer endorsements from co-founders and investors
  • Portfolio of ventures with status and traction data
  • Expertise tags validated by community engagement
The Platform

One app.
The whole ecosystem.
