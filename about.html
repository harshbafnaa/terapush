About Terapush

Built for the
builders of Bharat

We're building the infrastructure layer for India's startup ecosystem — a single platform that replaces fragmented tools, disconnected communities, and siloed networks.

Our Mission
To become the operating system of India's startup ecosystem — connecting every founder, investor, mentor, and creator in one intelligent, purpose-built platform.
The Problem We're Solving

India's startup ecosystem is exploding — but the infrastructure hasn't kept up. Founders juggle LinkedIn for networking, WhatsApp for deals, Twitter for signals, and email for investor outreach. There's no home base.

Terapush is that home base. A hybrid social, professional, and marketplace platform designed from the ground up for the Indian startup context — with the speed, nuance, and ambition that Bharat's builders deserve.

We're not trying to copy Silicon Valley. We're building for the next generation of Indian founders who are creating companies for the world.

What We Stand For

Our principles

01
Signal Over Noise

Every feature we build filters for quality. We believe in fewer, more meaningful connections — not vanity metrics.

02
India First

We're not localizing a Western product. We're building natively for the Indian context — the languages, the relationships, the pace.

03
Transparency

Verified profiles, honest deal flow, no hidden algorithms pushing paid content. What you see is what's real.

04
Access for All

Whether you're in Bengaluru or Bhopal, a first-time founder or a seasoned operator — Terapush gives you the same access.

05
Community Ownership

We build with our users, not for them. Founding members help define the platform's direction from day one.

06
Long-term Thinking

We're not optimizing for the next funding round. We're building something that will matter to Indian founders for decades.

Our Journey

Where we've
been & where we're going

Early 2025

Terapush Founded

Harsh, Jaivik, and Mansi come together in Bengaluru with a shared conviction: India's startup ecosystem needs its own operating system. The company is incorporated as Terapush India Private Limited.

Mid 2025

Platform Design & Architecture

Product vision finalized. Platform architecture scoped. Development begins with a focus on the core networking, marketplace, and intelligence pillars. Waitlist opens to founding members.

Late 2025

Seed Funding & Ecosystem Building

Applying for Startup India Seed Fund Scheme. Building the founding cohort of users — founders, investors, and creators who will shape the platform's direction from day one.

2026

Platform Launch

Terapush will go live for founding members, followed by a broader public launch. The OS for India's startup ecosystem will soon be open for business.
